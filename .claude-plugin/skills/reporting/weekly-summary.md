You are executing the `/weekly-summary` command for NOMAD v2.0. This command generates a comprehensive weekly summary of the threat landscape personalized to your organization.

## Command Execution

1. **Gather Weekly Data**: Collect threats from past 7 days
2. **Trend Analysis**: Identify patterns and emerging threats
3. **Crown Jewel Focus**: Highlight threats to critical systems
4. **Generate Summary**: Use threat-synthesizer for comprehensive report
